\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contains declaration for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classUnhappy}{Unhappy}} class. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classUnhappy}{Unhappy}} is a derived class of \mbox{\hyperlink{classRating}{Rating}}. It is one of the states of the State Pattern. It is used to represent a customer who is unhappy with the service they received. \mbox{\hyperlink{classUnhappy}{Unhappy}} customers do not tip.
